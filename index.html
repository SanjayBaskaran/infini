About Us

The second edition of Infinity an intra-college technical symposium is proudly presented by the Computational Sciences Association, Department of Applied Mathematics and Computational Sciences, PSG College of Technology on "13th, 14th and 15th February 2020". The symposium is filled with six exciting events for the first and second years of all B.E/B.Tech/B.Sc/M.Sc/MCA programmes.

Infinity brings the most significant platform for upbringing technical and non-technical skills of the first and second-year techies by providing them the key opportunity to showcase out-breaking talents in various events. We come up with events that are drafted to let out the team spirit, leadership capability and the desire to innovate.
